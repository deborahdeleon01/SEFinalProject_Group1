Name General Biology I
Description A study of the basic principles of Biology. Topics will include biological chemistry, cell structure and function, photosynthesis and respiration, DNA structure and function, mitosis, meiosis, Mendelian genetics, evolution and the structure and function of bacteria, viruses, protozoan, algae, fungi and plants. Credit Restriction: Credit may be received in only one of BIOL 1406 or BIOL 1487.
